FAQ

AI chatbot questions, answered.

How is a custom chatbot different from a tool like Intercom or Drift?

+
Those tools give you a platform to configure. We build a chatbot trained specifically on your business, your services, and your customers' actual questions. Built for your business, not for a generic platform.

Can the chatbot book appointments directly?

+
Yes. We connect it to your calendar system so visitors can pick a time and book without leaving the chat. Your team sees the appointment with full context on who the lead is and what they asked about.

What if the chatbot gets a question it can't answer?

+
It hands the conversation to your team with the full chat history. The visitor doesn't have to repeat themselves, and your team picks up exactly where the bot left off.

Do I need technical knowledge to manage the chatbot?

+
None. We handle the build, training, and deployment. We walk your team through the dashboard in under an hour. If you need updates to the bot's knowledge base later, email us and we handle it.
