+
Yes. Syntrex builds custom AI chatbots for small businesses that answer customer questions, qualify leads, book appointments, and hand conversations off to your team when needed. Every chatbot is trained on your services, pricing, and process so it sounds like your business — not a generic bot.

Can Syntrex help with workflow automation in Orlando?

+
Yes. We work with Orlando and Central Florida businesses to automate manual, repetitive workflows — lead follow-up, order processing, reporting, and internal operations. We design the automation, build it, hand it over, and make sure it runs reliably in the background without needing your team to babysit it.

Where is Syntrex LLC located?

+
Syntrex LLC is headquartered at 513 Main Street, Windermere, FL 34786. We serve clients throughout Windermere, Orlando, Winter Garden, Dr. Phillips, and the rest of Central Florida, and we work remotely with businesses across the United States.

How much does an AI automation or chatbot project cost?

+
Pricing depends on scope. Simple AI chatbots and focused workflow automations are priced as fixed-scope projects, while larger custom AI systems are quoted after a short discovery call. Unlike subscription tools, you own the system we build for your business — there are no recurring platform fees.

How long does it take to build a custom AI system?

+
Most AI chatbots and smaller workflow automations are delivered in days. Larger custom AI systems and internal tools typically ship in 2 to 4 weeks depending on integrations. We hand over fully working systems, ready to run from day one.

What industries does Syntrex work with?

+
Syntrex has shipped systems for CPG brands, industrial B2B companies, service businesses, and growing startups. Any Florida business that loses time to manual work — or wants to scale without adding headcount — is a good fit for our AI automation services.
